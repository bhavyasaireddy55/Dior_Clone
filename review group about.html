About the House of Dior

About Dior

Founded in 1946 by Christian Dior, the House of Dior has become a global symbol of elegance, luxury, and innovation in fashion. From haute couture to timeless fragrances, Dior represents the height of French sophistication.

Fashion

Dior is renowned for redefining femininity through exquisite tailoring, bold silhouettes, and couture craftsmanship. Each collection blends tradition with modern style.

Fragrances

Dior fragrances like Sauvage and Miss Dior have captivated the world with their unique scents. Each perfume is a statement of luxury and emotion.

Accessories

From iconic handbags to statement sunglasses and jewelry, Dior accessories reflect refined craftsmanship and timeless elegance.
